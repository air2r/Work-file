Our Unique Approach. Simplicity.

Our founders are well, landlords of four properties across two states and one of us happens to be a computer programmer! We started the company to create really good software that would help us manage our properties, communicate with our tenants and help keep our accounting in order for tax time. Our biggest issue was that some of the products on the market tried to appeal to everyone - so there was a steep learning curve or the cost squeezed on our already thin profit margin.

Our approach is different. We took the top features like Financials, Tenant Management and Communication and Portal Access and focused on those. Then, we leveraged our secure and redudant hosting partners and in-house developers to build the platform, ensuring almost up availability, redudant backups, and 100% secured connections ensursing your data is always safe and fully protected.
