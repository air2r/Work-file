our magic story

Chasing Rats Games was cofounded in summer 2018 by Alexis, Rémi and Denis, three passioned game designers. With our different skillset and our passion for multiplayer, we aim to create games that distinguish themselves by theirs uniques mechanics and multiplayer interactions.
We are currently working on our first game Struggling, a coop physic-based adventure where both player control one of the arm of an abomination.
