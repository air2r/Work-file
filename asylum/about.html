About
Indie Asylum

The Indie Asylum is an amalgamation of video game companies all found under one roof. The types of companies in the Asylum span from video game developers, marketing agencies, event organizers, gamification studios and VR game developers. We live and breathe gaming and we do it together. The Indie Asylum is more than just great f*cking games however, it’s a great f*cking lifestyle. When you join the Indie Asylum, you are also given the Asylum Prescription™ which consists of:

  • — Flexible hours ( 8-4, 9-5, 10-6)
  • — Sandwich Machine™ (Breakfast and Lunch at work)
  • — Group Insurance
  • — Gaming Nights (Every last Friday of the month, we party because we deserve it.)
  • — Access to La Guilde (280+ studios)
  • — Lunch and Learns (Different topics regarding gamedev, Masterclasses)
